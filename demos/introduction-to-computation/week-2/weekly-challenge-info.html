Week 2 Weekly challenge

Each week, I’m going to end our tutorial with a tougher challenge that you’ll need to solve in your pair programming groups. This challenge will put together all of the concepts we learned during that class.

Today’s challenge: a bank account simulator

Prompt

Your task is to create two functions that similate depositing and withdrawing money from a bank. Your functions should feature arguments for how much money you deposit or withdraw. You should use a variable to keep track of how much money you have. If you go below $0, your code should say you have no money left

Example:

  • Start with $100
  • Deposit $50
  • Withdraw $100
  • Withdraw $50
  • Your code should log something like “You’re out of money!”
