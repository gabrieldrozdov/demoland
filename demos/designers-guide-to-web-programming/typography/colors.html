There are a few ways to specify color in CSS:

  • Hex codes like #ff0000.
  • RGB (red, green, blue) codes like rgb(50,50,100).
    The numbers range from 0–255.
  • RGBA codes like rgb(50,50,100,.5).
    The “A” stands for “alpha” (aka opacity).
  • HSL codes like hsl(100deg,50%,50%).
    This stands for hue, saturation, and color.
    Hue is between 0–359deg, and saturation/color are percentages.
    You can also do HSLA codes just like RGBA codes.
  • Or, you can just specify color by its name, like blue.
    What colors are represented by name? It’s a bit random, but there are lists online.
